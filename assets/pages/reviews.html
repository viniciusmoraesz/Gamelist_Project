Synopsis

Forza Horizon 4 is an open-world racing game developed by Playground Games in collaboration with Turn 10 and published by Xbox Game Studios, which at the time of release was still known as Microsoft Studios. It is the fourth in the Forza Horizon franchise and the eleventh in the Forza edition. It was released on October 2, 2018 exclusively for Xbox One and Windows 10. Owners of the Supreme version of the game have 4 days early access.
